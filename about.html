About Us

Our Personalized Oncology Platform leverages advanced AI and machine learning to predict cancer types and recommend tailored treatments based on patient data and medical images. By analyzing lifestyle factors, environmental data, and clinical information, we provide actionable insights to improve treatment outcomes. This project aims to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare, empowering doctors and patients with data-driven decisions for better cancer care.
